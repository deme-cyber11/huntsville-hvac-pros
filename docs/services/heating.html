Heat Pumps: A Smart Choice for Alabama Homes

For most Huntsville homes, a heat pump is an excellent choice. In Alabama's mild winters (average January low: 32°F), heat pumps operate highly efficiently — providing both heating and cooling from a single system. Modern units with variable-speed compressors handle Huntsville's temperature swings smoothly. We install Carrier, Trane, and Lennox heat pumps rated for our climate zone.

Gas Furnace Services

Many Huntsville homes have natural gas furnaces — reliable, powerful, and cost-effective for heating when temperatures really drop. Our certified technicians service all major gas furnace brands and diagnose issues from ignitor failure to heat exchanger cracks. A cracked heat exchanger is a carbon monoxide risk — if you suspect one, call immediately.
