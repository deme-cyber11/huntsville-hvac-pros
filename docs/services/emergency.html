24/7 Emergency HVAC Service in Huntsville, AL

An AC failure at midnight in August in Huntsville isn't just uncomfortable — it can be dangerous. With overnight lows still in the 70s and heat indexes above 100°F during peak summer, a failed AC puts elderly family members, young children, and pets at serious risk. That's why our emergency line is answered by a live technician 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year — not a voicemail or call center.

Emergency Line: (256) 215-9287 — answered live, 24/7. We dispatch within hours, not days.

When to Call the Emergency Line

  • Complete AC failure during extreme heat (indoor temp rising above 85°F)
  • No heat during a cold snap — especially with elderly, infants, or pets
  • Suspected gas leak or carbon monoxide alarm triggered
  • Electrical burning smell from HVAC equipment
  • Water leak from AC causing active property damage
  • AC failure before an important event, guests, or medical need
