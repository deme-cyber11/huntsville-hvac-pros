Expert AC Repair in Huntsville, AL

When your AC fails in Huntsville's summer heat — with temperatures averaging 91°F and humidity that makes it feel even hotter — you need fast, reliable repair. Huntsville HVAC Pros dispatches licensed technicians the same day for most calls, with true 24/7 emergency response when the situation is urgent.

Our trucks arrive stocked with the most common replacement parts for Carrier, Trane, Lennox, Goodman, and Rheem systems — meaning we often complete repairs on the first visit without having to order parts.

Common AC Problems We Fix

  • AC not cooling — refrigerant leaks, dirty coils, compressor failure
  • Frozen evaporator coils — very common in Alabama's high humidity
  • Capacitor and contactor failure — the most common summer breakdown
  • Blower motor failure — no airflow from vents
  • Refrigerant recharge (R-410A and legacy R-22 systems)
  • Thermostat malfunctions and wiring issues
  • Drainage problems — clogged condensate lines
  • Unusual noises — banging, squealing, rattling
  • Short cycling — system turns on and off too frequently

Our Repair Process

We arrive on time, diagnose the problem thoroughly, and give you a written quote before touching anything. If the repair doesn't make financial sense compared to replacement, we'll tell you honestly — and walk you through your options without pressure. Our job is to give you the right answer, not the most expensive one.

AC Repair Costs in Huntsville

Most common repairs range from $75–$600. Capacitor replacement: $150–$350. Refrigerant recharge: $200–$400. Major component failure (compressor, evaporator coil) may warrant system replacement — we give you the full picture so you can decide what's right for your situation.
