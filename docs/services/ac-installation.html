Why Proper Sizing Matters in Alabama

Many HVAC contractors install oversized systems — it's one of the most common mistakes in the industry. An oversized AC in Huntsville will cool your home quickly but never run long enough to remove humidity properly. Result: your home feels cool but clammy, mold risk increases, and the system fails early from constant short-cycling. We perform a Manual J load calculation on every installation.
