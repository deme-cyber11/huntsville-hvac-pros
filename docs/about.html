Our Story

Huntsville's HVAC Team You Can Trust

Founded in 2009, Huntsville HVAC Pros has been keeping Madison County homes cool in summer and warm in winter — with honest service and zero pressure tactics.

Who We Are

Huntsville HVAC Pros is a locally owned heating and air conditioning company serving Huntsville, Alabama and all of Madison County. We were built on a simple belief: HVAC service should be honest, fast, and fairly priced.

We started with one truck and two technicians in 2009 — back when Huntsville was smaller and everyone knew everyone. Today we run a full fleet across Madison County, but our culture hasn't changed. Every tech we hire passes background checks, holds Alabama HVAC licensing, and is trained to fix the problem — not sell you something you don't need.

Why Huntsville Chose Us

North Alabama's climate is tough on HVAC systems. Summers average 91°F with high humidity — your AC runs for months straight. Winters bring unexpected cold snaps where furnaces need to work without warning. Our technicians are trained specifically for this region's demands.

We know the difference between a system that needs a $95 refrigerant charge versus one that needs a $4,000 replacement — and we'll be straight with you about which is which. That honesty is why 300+ Huntsville homeowners have left us five-star reviews.

Our Licensing & Credentials

We are fully licensed HVAC contractors under the Alabama Licensing Board for General Contractors — the state authority that regulates HVAC work in Alabama. Every technician holds current Alabama HVAC certification, and we carry comprehensive general liability and workers' compensation insurance.

What this means for you: if anything goes wrong during service at your home, you're protected. You're not covered when you hire an unlicensed tech from a social media ad.

Brands We Trust

We install and service all major HVAC brands used across North Alabama:

  • Carrier — industry leader with superior reliability ratings for humid climates
  • Trane — "It's hard to stop a Trane" for good reason; we're authorized dealers
  • Lennox — high-efficiency units with excellent SEER ratings for Alabama summers
  • Goodman — reliable value brand with strong warranty programs
  • Rheem, Daikin, York — plus all other residential brands

Our Guarantee

Every job comes with our satisfaction guarantee. If you're not 100% happy with the work, call us and we'll make it right — no argument, no charge. That's how we've earned over 300 five-star reviews in Huntsville.
